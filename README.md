## Problem Statement
Yulu wants to understand the factors affecting demand for shared electric cycles in India.

## Objective
Identify whether bike rental demand depends on:
- Working day
- Season
- Weather conditions

## Techniques Used
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- 2-Sample T-Test
- One-Way ANOVA
- Chi-Square Test of Independence

## Key Insights
- Working day has no significant impact on demand
- Demand varies significantly across seasons
- Weather conditions strongly affect rentals
- Weather is dependent on season

## Business Recommendations
- Increase fleet during summer and fall
- Use weather-based demand forecasting
- Optimize maintenance during low-demand periods
